Privacy Policy

Nawah — Intelligent Family Organization

Effective date: 1 May 2026  ·  Last updated: 1 May 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Nawah ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use the Nawah mobile application. By creating an account or using Nawah, you agree to the practices described below.

Nawah is a family-organization app. Most data you create — chat messages, calendar events, tasks, lists, announcements, polls, vault items, and shared location — is shared with the other members of the families you join. You should treat anything you post inside a family as visible to every member of that family.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide directly

  • Account information. Your email address, password (stored only as a one-way hash by Firebase Authentication), and display name. A profile photo is optional.
  • Family content. Chat messages, photos, videos, voice notes, calendar events, tasks, shared lists, polls, announcements, vault items (photos, PDFs, videos, rich-text notes), and any other content you create inside a family.
  • Reports and blocks. If you report a message or block another user, we record that action so our moderation team can review it and so blocking can be enforced.

2.2 Information collected automatically

  • Device information. Operating system, app version, language, and a Firebase Cloud Messaging token used solely to send push notifications you have enabled.
  • Approximate and precise location. Only when you have enabled the family map feature and granted "When In Use" location permission. Location is shared with your family while the app is open and is not collected in the background.
  • Authentication metadata. Sign-in timestamps and a Firebase user ID used to associate you with your data.

2.3 Information we do not collect

  • We do not track you across other apps or websites. The app's privacy manifest declares NSPrivacyTracking = false.
  • We do not use third-party advertising networks.
  • We do not use Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics, or any third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not collect your phone number, postal address, contacts, date of birth, gender, or browsing history.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and authenticate your account.
  • To deliver the app's core features — sharing content with the families you join, syncing events to your device calendar, displaying your family on the map, and processing your requests to the AI Assistant.
  • To send you push notifications you have opted in to (chat messages, task assignments, calendar reminders, announcements, polls).
  • To enforce our Terms of Service, including reviewing reported content and acting on user blocks.
  • To diagnose technical problems and prevent fraud and abuse.

5. How Your Information Is Shared

5.1 With other family members

Content you create inside a family — messages, events, tasks, lists, polls, announcements, vault items, and your live location while the family map is enabled — is visible to every member of that family. Your display name and profile photo are visible to other members so they can identify you in shared content.

5.2 With service providers

We use the following providers to operate the app. Each receives only the data needed to deliver its service:

ProviderPurposeData shared
Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Storage, Cloud Functions, App Check, Cloud Messaging) Account authentication, primary database, push notifications, abuse prevention. Your account information, your family content, your device's FCM token.

5.3 We do not sell your data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for advertising or marketing.

5.4 Legal disclosures

We may disclose your information if required by law, valid legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or others. We will resist requests we believe to be overbroad and will notify affected users where legally permitted.

6. Data Storage and Security

  • Account data and family content are stored in Google Firebase (Firestore and Cloud Storage), encrypted at rest by Google.
  • Vault file uploads are stored in Cloudflare R2, encrypted at rest.
  • All network communication uses TLS (HTTPS).
  • App Check is enabled on production builds to reduce abuse from unauthorized clients.
  • We do not implement custom or proprietary cryptography. The app qualifies for the standard U.S. encryption export exemption.

No system is perfectly secure. While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Push Notifications

We send push notifications via Firebase Cloud Messaging for events such as new chat messages, task assignments, calendar reminders, announcements, and polls. Notification payloads contain a short title and body (truncated to 120 characters) and the IDs needed to open the right screen. You can disable notifications globally in your device settings or per-category in Settings → Notifications inside the app.

8. Your Choices and Rights

  • Access and correction. You can view and edit your profile from inside the app at any time.
  • Account deletion. You can delete your account from Settings → Account → Delete Account. Deletion permanently removes your profile, blocked list, notification preferences, FCM tokens, your shared location, and your family memberships, and deletes your Firebase Authentication record. Messages you sent and items you added to family vaults remain visible to the family but will no longer show your name. Family creators must transfer or delete their families before deleting their account; the app guides you through this.
  • Reporting and blocking. You can report any chat message via long-press, and you can block any family member at any time. Blocked users can be reviewed and unblocked from Settings → Blocked Users.
  • Permission control. You can revoke camera, microphone, photo, calendar, location, and notification permissions from your device's system settings at any time. The app will continue to work with reduced functionality.

9. Data Retention

We retain your account information and family content for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we delete the items listed in section 8 immediately. Content you contributed to families you have left or to families that continue to exist after your deletion is retained as part of those families' shared history; it is no longer attributed to your account once your user record is removed.

Vault file access URLs expire automatically after 24 hours and are regenerated on demand.

10. International Transfers

Our service providers (Google and Cloudflare) operate globally, and your data may be processed in countries other than the one in which you reside, including the United States. Each provider applies its own safeguards for cross-border transfers, including, where applicable, the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses.

11. Children

Nawah is intended for use by adults and families together. The app is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under that age. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information without parental consent, please contact us at the address below and we will delete it.
